About Nike

Welcome to the world of Nike, where innovation meets style, and every step is a leap towards excellence. Since our founding in 1964 by the visionary duo Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, Nike has transformed the landscape of athletic wear and sports fashion.

Our mission is simple: to inspire and innovate, making every athlete's journey exceptional. If you have a body, consider yourself an athlete—empowering you is at the core of what we do.

"Just do it." It's not just a slogan; it's a philosophy that fuels our drive. Nike isn't just a brand; it's a symbol of courage, determination, and the relentless pursuit of greatness.

Join us in celebrating the joy of sport, the power of community, and the endless possibilities that lie ahead. Thank you for choosing Nike—where every step is a statement, and every leap is a victory.
